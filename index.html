What I do

On my educational and professional background, I´m practical and result-oriented person, always looking not just for answers but the right questions to ask. In this way, on my research and work-related projects I focus on three stages: Data wrangling, Analytics and Results Presentations.

Data wrangling I have a virtual machine where I do all my work but if it’s needed. My main cloud providers are GCP and AWS where I do all my jobs and pipelines.
Analytics In this field, my statistics, machine learning and econometrics comes as my analytics toolbox. When I do models, I always try to be robust but, as a economist, I’m aware that statistical significance is not enough, sometimes theory must be implemented as well.
Results Presentation I think that soft skills really matters for presentation, sometimes more than data wrangling and analytics. In this section, I train myself writing blogs, giving speeches and explain everything that I do to anyone.

Overall, if you want to see my profile, check my LinkedIn account to see my latest achievements. Also check, my personal projects and Blogs where I research and discuss topics that I found interesting.
